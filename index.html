Depict

List, Explore, Collect & Play

Depict is art made individual. By creating your own collections, you represnt you unique personality. Much like the art pieces, the logo is dynamic, taking multiple forms to fit different situations.

The brand represents the uniqueness of artists and collectors alike and suggests a moving piece, unlike the static art world of the past.

Logo

Designed to be dynamic

The logo is comprised of simple shapes that abstract towards play, letter d & letter p.

Deconstructed, the polygons can be used as repeating or dynamic elements throughout the brand experience.
